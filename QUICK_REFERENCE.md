## 🎯 NPC QUEST TYPES

### Town NPCs:
- Themed to city specialty
- Medium to Hard difficulty
- Better rewards (100-500 gold)

### Settlement NPCs:
- General tasks (pest control, gathering)
- Easy to Medium difficulty
- Moderate rewards (40-150 gold)

### Camp NPCs:
- Quick tasks
- Easy difficulty
- Small rewards (30-60 gold)

## 💬 NPC DIALOGUE INDICATORS

- **No icon** = General conversation only
