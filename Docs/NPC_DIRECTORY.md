## Overview
Complete listing of all NPCs across towns, settlements, and camps in the game world.

## 🏰 GreyWolf Town (Main Hub) - 24 NPCs

### Central Square (3 NPCs)
- **Harold the Town Crier** (Informant) - Announces news and events
- **Old Tom** (Elder) - Fountain keeper with stories of the past
- **Rosie the Flower Seller** (Merchant) - Sells flowers and trinkets

### Crafters District - North Side (4 NPCs)
- **Bjorn Ironhammer** (Questgiver) ❗ - Master blacksmith at The Rusty Anvil
  - Quest: "Forge Master's Request" - Collect 5 iron ingots
- **Talia Softhand** (Merchant) - Expert leather worker at Tanner's Workshop
- **Gemma Goldweaver** (Merchant) - Skilled jeweler at Golden Trinkets
- **Young Marcus** (Citizen) - Apprentice running errands

### Service Quarter - East Side (5 NPCs)
- **Martha Warmheart** (Citizen) - Kind innkeeper at The Cozy Inn
- **Cornelius Goldvault** (Merchant) - Banker at GreyWolf Bank & Storage
- **Guild Master Varen** (Questgiver) ❗ - Experienced guild master
  - Quest: "Guild Initiation" - Defeat 10 monsters
- **Scarlett Redmark** (Informant) - Tracks bounties at Hunter's Lodge
- **Master Swordsman Drake** (Questgiver) ❗ - Legendary trainer at Training Hall
  - Quest: "Warrior's Trial" - Explore 3 dangerous areas

### Mystic Quarter - South Side (4 NPCs)
- **Wizard Aldous** (Merchant) - Eccentric wizard at Arcane Emporium
- **Healer Lyanna** (Merchant) - Gentle healer at Healing Hands
- **Historian Eldric** (Elder) - Keeper of the Hall of Fame
- **Champion Seraphina** (Elder) 🏆 - Oversees ascension path (Lv 25+)

### Entertainment District - West Side (3 NPCs)
- **Lucky Fingers** (Merchant) - Charismatic host at The Lucky Dragon
- **Fauna Wildwhisper** (Merchant) - Beast tamer at Pet Stable
- **Silk Silverthimble** (Merchant) - Elegant elven tailor at Fine Threads

### General Town NPCs (5 NPCs)
- **Captain Ironshield** (Guard) ❗ - Town guard captain
  - Quest: "Town Defense" - Eliminate 6 bandits
- **Guard Brennan** (Guard) - Friendly patrol guard
- **Mysterious Wanderer** (Traveler) - Hooded figure with secrets
- **Betty Sweetbread** (Citizen) - Plump baker
- **Scholar Percival** (Informant) - Runs town library
- **Jack Haymaker** (Citizen) - Stable hand

## ⛺ Camps (1 NPC each)

Each of the 20 camps has one NPC:
- Weary Traveler, Camp Keeper, Patrol Guard, or Fellow Adventurer
