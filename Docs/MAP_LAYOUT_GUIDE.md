## 💡 DISCOVERY MECHANICS

### Reveal Radius by Location Type
- **Major Towns:** 15-tile radius (discovers nearby settlements/camps)
- **Settlements:** 8-tile radius (discovers local camps)
- **Camps:** Current location only

### Progressive Unlocking
1. Start at Havenbrook (center) - reveals nearby locations
2. Travel to discovered locations
3. Major towns unlock entire regions
4. Story progression reveals distant cities
5. Natural exploration fills in the gaps

### Exploration Statistics
- **44 Total Locations** to discover
- **~34% discovered** at game start (Havenbrook + nearby)
- **Full exploration** achievement at 100%
