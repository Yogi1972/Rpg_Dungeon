# ⚔️ RPG Dungeon Crawler

A classic text-based dungeon-crawling RPG featuring turn-based combat, character progression, quest systems, and local multiplayer support.

![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.0.0-blue)
![.NET](https://img.shields.io/badge/.NET-10-purple)
![C#](https://img.shields.io/badge/C%23-14.0-green)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-orange)

## 🎮 Features

- **🗡️ Four Unique Classes**: Warrior, Mage, Rogue, and Priest
- **🧙 Multiple Races**: Human, Elf, Dwarf, and Gnome
- **⚔️ Turn-Based Combat**: D20 dice roll mechanics (like D&D)
- **🏰 Dungeon Exploration**: Procedurally generated dungeons
- **📜 Quest System**: Dynamic quests and bounty hunting
- **🎯 Achievement System**: Track your accomplishments
- **🎒 Equipment & Inventory**: Weapons, armor, accessories, and consumables
- **🌟 Skill Trees**: Unlock powerful passive abilities
- **🐾 Pet Companions**: Adopt pets with special abilities
- **⚒️ Crafting System**: Create items and brew potions
- **🌦️ Weather & Time Systems**: Dynamic world conditions
- **💰 Economy**: Banking, trading, and shopping
- **👥 Local Multiplayer**: Play with friends on the same PC
- **💾 Save System**: Multiple save slots (up to 10)
- **📊 Error Logging**: Comprehensive error reporting and diagnostics

4. Choose "Start New Game" from the main menu
5. Create your character and begin your adventure!

## 🎲 Game Mechanics

### Combat System
- Turn-based combat using d20 rolls
- Natural 20 = Critical Hit!
- Natural 1 = Critical Miss!
- Use attacks, abilities, or items each turn

### Character Progression
- Gain XP from combat to level up
- Unlock skill trees with powerful abilities
- Equip better weapons and armor
- Complete quests for rewards

### Dungeon Exploration
- Use torches to light dark dungeons
- Discover loot and treasure chests
- Face increasingly difficult enemies
- Navigate through multiple dungeon levels

## 📖 How to Play

Detailed instructions are available in-game through the "How to Play" option in the main menu.

### Quick Start Tips
- Save often at camp (option 3 in main menu)
- Balance your party with different classes
- Keep equipment repaired at the blacksmith
- Complete easier quests first to build strength
- Use torches when exploring dungeons
